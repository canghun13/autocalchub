Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

AutoCalcHub ("we," "our," or "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use our site, how it is used, and your options regarding that information.

Information We Do Not Collect

AutoCalcHub does not collect personal information. All calculations performed on our tools are processed entirely within your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any values you enter into our calculators. No account creation or login is required to use any feature of this site.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This service collects anonymized data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country/region level)
  • Device type and browser
  • How visitors arrived at the site (search, direct, referral)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored through Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. This is known as interest-based or personalized advertising.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit Google's partner sites policy.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies — placed by Google Analytics to track usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to enable personalized or interest-based advertising

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features or the ads you see. Most modern browsers provide controls to manage cookie preferences.

Third-Party Links

Our blog posts and tool pages may contain links to external websites. These sites have their own privacy policies, which we do not control and are not responsible for. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

Children's Privacy

AutoCalcHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through our site, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
